What is it ?

The Repos Ranking Feature sorts repositories based on key metrics like stars, forks, contributions, and activity. It helps users discover trending or high-quality repositories by providing a ranked list, often with filtering options based on language, timeframe, and engagement levels.

How to use

Users can explore ranked repositories by selecting filters such as programming language, timeframe (daily, weekly, monthly), and ranking criteria (stars, forks, contributions, activity). They can browse the list to discover trending projects, click on a repository for details, and access its GitHub page for further exploration.
